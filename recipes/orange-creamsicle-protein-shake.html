ORANGE CREAMSICLE PROTEIN SHAKE

This orange creamsicle protein shake is reminiscent of the famous ice cream bar. It's tasty, healthy, and sure to keep you full until lunch or beyond.

Ingredients

  • 1 orange - zested, peeled, and separated into segments
  • 1 cup soy milk
  • 1 (5.3 ounce) container plain Greek yogurt
  • 5 ice cubes
  • 1 scoop vanilla protein powder
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Smoothie ingredients including oranges, yogurt, and ice cubes.
  3. Combine orange segments, zest, soy milk, yogurt, ice, protein powder, and vanilla in a blender.
  4. Smoothie ingredients layered inside a personal blender cup.
  5. Blend until smooth.
